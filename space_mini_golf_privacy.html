Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Space Mini Golf collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Ads and Third-Party Services

This game displays ads provided by third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob. These services may collect data including:

  • Device information (e.g. device model, OS version)
  • Advertising ID
  • App usage data
  • IP address and general location

This data is collected for the purpose of showing relevant ads and measuring performance.

2. Data Collection and Use

Space Mini Golf does not collect or store any personal data directly. However, ad providers may collect data as described above.

3. Children's Privacy

If your child is under 13, we recommend reviewing this privacy policy and supervising their gameplay. Ad providers may collect data in compliance with COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act), but no personal data is collected by us directly.

4. No Account Required

You can play the game without creating an account or providing any personal information.

5. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Please check back periodically for updates.
